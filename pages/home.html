MikuFan’s Snow Miku 2019 Photo Report

Hello, Miku and Snow Miku fans! It’s been a long time coming, but we’ve finally managed to sort through our collection of photos to bring you our photo report of Snow Miku 2019 and its various activities! This year was made extra special, largely in part to it also being the 10th anniversary of Snow Miku.
Good Smile Company Highlights Stunning Lineup of Latest Figures!

Good Smile Company is constantly coming out with great figures - so many, in fact, that it can be hard to keep up with! That’s what’s so great about photo sessions, though, and Good Smile Company recently held one filled with tons of gorgeous figures. Wish you could've snuck a peek? Here's your chance!
Astonishingly Detailed 3D GUMI Etched in Crystal!

Vocaloid characters are hugely popular the world over. It seems like everyone loves Miku. Many also support the Megpoid image character, GUMI. Using 3D data created by Mamama, an extremely active creator on MikuMikuDance, fans can make GUMI move precisely on point with any dance they choose.
Wonder Festival 2018 Summer: Industry Edition

Wonder Festival, the world's biggest figure festival, held its summer event on Jul. 29 at Makuhari Messe in Chiba. With tons of upcoming releases and amateur creations displayed for fans to admire (for free!), there was something to see for every genre.
Hatsune Miku Is Here to Heal Your Computer Fatigue!

A plush computer cushion set of the virtual singer Hatsune Miku is available for pre-order now on Bandai’s official shopping site, Premium Bandai. Pre-orders for this Premium Bandai exclusive opened at 11:00 am on Wednesday, June 15, and the cushion set costs 6,264 yen (tax included, shipping and handling separate).
Valentine's Day at Miku | Miku Waterfront

Woo your valentine this year with our Valentine’s Day Aburi Prime Kaiseki. This elevated dining experience features four exquisite courses designed to complement our signature Aburi Prime, as well as a thoughtfully curated premium beverage pairing. Our special Valentine’s Day menu is available February 12th – 14th. View the full menu here, and book now to secure your reservation with us.
Hatsune Miku Embarks on First Ever European Tour!

Vocaloid sensation Hatsune Miku is set to tour Europe for the very first time! Hatsune Miku Expo 2018 Europe kicks off on Dec. 1 in Paris, France before heading to Cologne, Germany on Dec. 4 and London, United Kingdom on Dec. 8. The Paris date is set to coincide with the wider “Japonismes 2018” project celebrating Japanese and French relations throughout 2018.
EV Bike Company Mirai to Use Hatsune Miku Collaborative Bike in This Year’s Isle of Man TT

Mirai, an electric motorcycle development company based in Ichinomiya, Aichi, announced its participation in this year’s Isle of Man TT race on March 19 at the Akiba Motor Show Cafe. The Isle of Man TT is an overseas road race that takes place on the British-governed Isle of Man. It is a legendary race that has been held since 1904. This year, it will be held from May 31-June 8.
Racing Miku Heads Towards the Finish Line With Adorably Cheeky

The 2018 version of Racing Miku is pulling into first place with a dynamic 1/7th scale figure manufactured by Good Smile Racing! Based on an illustration by Kanzaki Hiro, the figure features Miku in an exciting pose as she hurtles toward the finish line. She’s about to pass on her way to a first place win!
Snow Miku Takes Over Sapporo For SNOW MIKU 2019!

We went to SNOW MIKU 2019, held during the same period as the Sapporo Snow Festival! Tokyo Otaku Mode’s biggest Hatsune Miku fan lives in Sapporo and went to take photos and write this report for everyone!
Hatsune Miku Takes Center Stage with Kabuki-Themed Figure!

Vocaloid diva Hatsune Miku is embracing traditional culture with a fantastically detailed figure from Good Smile Company! The figure features Miku in the role of courtesan Keisei Hatsune Dayu in the kabuki play Kuruwa Kotoba Awase Kagami, which she performed at the 2017 Niconico Chokaigi.
BZITA’s Hatsune Miku Mobius Ring Now Available for Preorder

BZITA, a maker of anime-themed accessories and jewelry, has announced the upcoming release for their Hatsune Miku mobius ring. The ring takes subtle design accents from the skirt of Hatsune Miku’s V4X outfit design, and has mobius curve to signify infinity. Now you can have a ring to symbolize your infinite love for Miku!
Hatsune Miku Symphony 2017 Sets Vocaloid to Full Orchestra!

Hatsune Miku Symphony 2017 was held on Nov. 29 in Tokyo, and it gave the audience a chance to hear their favorite Vocaloid songs backed by a full orchestra!The night opened with "Mirai Jokyoku", the symphony theme song produced by producer Mitchie M. You can hear a short version of it below.
“Mai Waifu” on Cars?! More than 1,000 Cars on Display at Itasha Festival Ita G Festa

The Eighth Ita G Festa, a festival for cars decorated with anime and game characters that are also referred to as itasha, was held on Sept. 22 in Odaiba Rainbow Town. There were about 1,020 itasha in total gathered from all around Japan! The best part was when all the imaginatively decorated cars were lined up on a huge site.
Hatsune Miku and Kizuna AI Set To Perform Together at Live Event in Osaka!

Virtual stars Hatsune Miku and Kizuna AI are set to take to the stage for a collaborative performance at TOKYO GIRLS COLLECTION Super Live -MATSURI-! TGC-Matsuri- is a stylish live entertainment show that will offer audiences a new experience thanks to the crossover of real and virtual elements.
