NEWS UPDATE
Hatsune Miku: Magical Mirai 2019 Official Album Crossfade Released

The crossfade preview for the Hatsune Miku: Magical Mirai 2019 official album is now up on the official Hatsune Miku YouTube channel! The album features the following 10 tracks, which will be part of the upcoming live concert experience in Osaka and Tokyo. Be sure to get in your practice for the cheer calls!
IO DATA Japan Announces Hatsune Miku X CDReco CD Drive Collaboration

IO DATA Japan has announced they will be collborating with Hatsune Miku to create a special themed version of their CDReco smartphone optical drive, called the “MIKUReco”. The drive is planned to be made first available at Magical Mirai 2019 in Osaka and Tokyo. The product design is still yet to be revealed, but should be made available soon.
Hatsune Miku Themed Gaming Chair and PC Case Teased in TSUKUMO’s 99magazine

We’ve been wondering if these Hatsune Miku gaming accessories would ever see the light of day! It appears the Hatsune Miku gaming accessories by TSUKUMO and Thermaltake, seen at their “Hatsune Miku game room” exhibit at Niconico Chokaigi, will be coming to market!
Washin Palette Announces Hatsune Miku PC Glasses in Metal Frames, Preorders Open July 7th

The latest Hatsune Miku collaboration yet with Washin has brought us a twin pair of full rim & under rim PC glasses in metal frames, with the main visual done by the illustrator Yunkel! Each models is priced at 16,092 yen (tax included), and will soon be available for preoder starting from July 7th at 13:00 JST.
Chiba City Monorail Announces “Miku Flyer” Collaboration for Magical Mirai 2019

Chiba City Monorail has announced they will be collaborating with Magical Mirai again this year with another “Miku Flyer” edition vehicle. Following the success of last year’s collaboration, Chiba City Monorail plans to significantly extend the service of this year’s collaboration! The main visual art for this year’s collaboration was drawn by Kurojishi.
TSUKUMO Announces Hatsune Miku Themed Acer & Dell Laptop Computers

Japanese PC and electronics retailer TSUKUMO has just unveiled their latest collaboration: Hatsune Miku styled laptop computers from Acer and Dell! These laptops are available for limited preorder within Japan starting today, and will ship on July 12th.
Rohto Announces Digi-Eye x Hatsune Miku Final Chapter “Last Promise with Miku”

It’s been 5 years since Rohto’s Digi-Eye eye drop launched a collaboration with Hatsune Miku, and it’s all coming to an end this month. Starting from July onward, Rohto’s Digi-Eye packaging will undergo a new design and the Miku design will be discontinued along with the AR app. For this one last farewell, and as thanks for continued support from all the Miku fans, Rohto has prepared us final farewell gifts.
